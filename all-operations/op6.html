Operation Tzuk Eitan was a large-scale military operation led by the IDF in the Gaza Strip, during 50 days of fighting between Israel and Hamas and other terrorist organizations. The operation was announced following the IDF's "Operation Return of Brothers" after the kidnapping and murder of the three Israeli boys on June 12 of that year, and the murder of the Palestinian boy Muhammad Abu Khadir in Jerusalem.
