In Operation Litani, the IDF entered the southern part of Lebanon up to the Litani River, and held this area for about three months, until it withdrew back to the international border. The purpose of the operation was to damage the infrastructure of the Palestinian terrorist organizations in southern Lebanon, thus reducing their activity against the State of Israel and its citizens. The Israeli army had 18 dead, 113 wounded, 4 more soldiers were killed, the Palestinian terrorist organizations had about 300 dead.
