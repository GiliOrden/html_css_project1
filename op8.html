Operation Dawn

August 5, 2022 - August 7, 2022

Operation Dawn was a military operation by the IDF in the Gaza Strip against the Palestinian Islamic Jihad. The IDF launched the operation with a targeted counterattack of Taysir al-Jabari, the commander of the northern division of the Palestinian Islamic Jihad in the northern Gaza Strip. The next day, Khaled Mansour, the commander of the southern division, was also killed. Throughout the operation, the Israeli Air Force attacked targets identified with The Palestinian Islamic Jihad in the Gaza Strip, including munitions warehouses and military positions, rocket launching facilities and other military targets. On the Israeli side, a "special situation in the home front" was implemented within 80 km of the Gaza Strip, and residents of the Gaza Strip were instructed to stay near protected areas.
