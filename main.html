THE ISRAELI'S WARS

Since its declaration of independence in May 1948, the State of Israel has fought eight recognized wars with its neighbouring Arab states, two major Palestinian Arab uprisings known as the First Intifada and the Second Intifada , and a broad series of other armed engagements rooted in the Arab–Israeli conflict.
