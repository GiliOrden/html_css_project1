Operation Pillar of Defense was a military operation by the IDF in the Gaza Strip. The operation began with the targeted assassination of Ahmed Jabari, the acting commander of the military wing of Hamas. During the operation, thousands of attacks were conducted on targets in the Gaza Strip, and at the same time about 1,500 were shot from the Gaza Strip Rockets were fired at the southern settlements and for the first time, firing was also carried out at the central cities including Rishon Lezion, Tel Aviv and Jerusalem. The operation ended a week later with the declaration of a cease-fire. The operation did not include a ground entry into the Strip and was mainly conducted by the Air Force and to a limited extent, the Navy, the Artillery Corps and other units .
