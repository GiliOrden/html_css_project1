Operation Wall Guardion

May 10, 2021 - May 21, 2021

Operation Wall Guardian was a large-scale military operation by the IDF in the Gaza Strip, after the Palestinian terrorist organizations led by Hamas began firing massive rockets at Jerusalem during the Flag Parade and later also at Gush Dan, the Lowlands, the Negev and the Sharon. The IDF attacked over 16,500 targets Terrorism, including: high-rise buildings that served as headquarters for Hamas and the Palestinian Islamic Jihad (PA), tens to hundreds of homes and offices of terrorist operatives, over 100 kilometers of underground tunnels, from the network of tunnels dug under the Gaza Strip, IDF warehouses and rocket depots, rocket production sites, rocket launchers and underground launch pits, anti-tank launchers, unmanned aerial vehicles, outposts, intelligence and technology assets, targets of the Hamas naval force and dozens of launch squads. According to Israeli sources.
